Korvstroganoff

Description

Korvstroganoff is a easy to cook and very tasty sausage stew with things like falukorb, onion, cream, tomato puree that works great for the everyday dinner. serve the korvstroganoff with cooket rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 portions of rice
  • 550g falukorv
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 msk cooking oil
  • 3 msk tomato puree
  • 2 1/2 dl cooking cream
  • 1 dl milk
  • 1 msk japanese soy
  • 1 tsk dijonmustard
  • peppar
  • salt
