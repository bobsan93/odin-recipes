Pancakes

Description

A Swedish classic. Pancakes fryed to a nice golden and served with raspberrys and maybe some whipped cream on top. Good for both dessert and dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package raspberrys (about 250 g)
  • 2 1/2dl Flour
  • 6 dl Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 tsk Salt
  • 2 msk Butter
